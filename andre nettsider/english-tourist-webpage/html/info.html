How to get there

From Fort William

  • By car to Mallaig, ferry to Armadale

  • By car over the Skye Bridge

  • By bus to Portree

  • By train to Mallaig, ferry to Armadale


From Inverness

  • By car to the Kyle of Lochalsh

  • By bus to Portree

  • By car/bus/train to Fort William


From Glasgow

  • By car/bus/train via Fort William

  • By bus to Portree

  • By train to Mallaig, ferry to Armadale


From Edinburgh

  • By car/bus/train via Fort William

  • By car/train to Inverness

  • By car/bus/train to Glasgow


From the Outer Hebrides

  • By ferry


From London

  • By car/train to Fort William


From outside of the UK

  • By plane to Glasgow/Fort William
