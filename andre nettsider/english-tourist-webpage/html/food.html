Dining options

The Three Chimneys

The Three Chimneys is a multiple award-winning restaurant near Loch Dunvegan in the northwest of Isle of Skye.
They offer many local dishes, like Dunvegan crab, flambadou oyster, roasted scallop, slow cooked lamb heart, cured monkfish and vatten croft beef.

The Dunvegan Restaurant

The Dunvegan Restraurant offer meals inspired by family-influenced Argentine cuisine and traditions
Their ever-chaning menu gives you a taste of Twice Dived Sconser Scallops, 6-week aged steak, rare breed Scottish pork, Highland and Island lamb; local lobster, langoustines and oysters.

The Ferry Inn

The Ferry Inn is a lively and stylish, 19th century, family-run inn.
Food at the Ferry Inn makes the most of local produce, like fresh seafood or vegetables. Some of the meals they offer include scallops, croquettes, lobsters, crabs and steak.


read more

The Oyster Shed

Being neither a restaurant nor a cafe, this big, draughty shed has become a Skye destination.
The Oyster Shed gives you an opportunity to try local seafood, including grilled lobsters with garlic butter, hot-smoked trout, cold-smoked salmon, dressed crab, kippers, and scampi.
