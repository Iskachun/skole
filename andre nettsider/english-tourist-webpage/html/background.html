Historical artifacts

Fossils and Dinosaur Footprints

Dinosaur footprints can be found on the shore at Staffin, and there are fossils from Skye on display in the Dinosaur Museum near Staffin.

Brochs

Brochs are small, round towers built around 2000 years ago and are scattered all over Skye.
The most accessible broch is Dun Beag at Struan.

Clans and Castles

There were two main clans on Skye: MacLeods and MacDonalds. The MacLeods own the Dunvegan Castle, which is the oldest inhabited castle in Scotland. The MacDonalds moved around a lot, from Dunsgaith (Sleat) to Duntulm (Trotternish peninsula), and then to Armadale.

Villages

There are many ruins of abandoned houses and villages all over Skye. Some were left voluntarily because the homes were too far away from transport or schools, while others were forcibly cleared by the landlords during the Highland Clearances.
