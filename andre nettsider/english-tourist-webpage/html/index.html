The Isle of Skye

- The largest island in the Inner Hebrides of Scotland.

National symbol: the thistle.

National animal: the unicorn.

National motto: Nemo me impune lacessit (no one harms me unpunished).

"Skye" comes from the word "Skuy" which means cloud or mist.

The landscapes have been used in many films and TV shows like Outlander and Transformers.

The Skye Bridge that connects the Isle of Skye to the mainland is over 1500 meters long
